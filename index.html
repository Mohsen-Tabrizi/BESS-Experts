About BESS Experts

BESS Experts is a media and knowledge platform created to give the Battery Energy Storage industry a clear and technically honest voice. The focus is on real applications, real challenges and real experience, not marketing buzzwords.

The host combines more than 20 years in electrical engineering, photovoltaics and BESS with TÜV certified expertise and hands on EPC and commissioning work. The goal is simple: make it easier for manufacturers, EPCs, investors and operators to understand each other and to build better projects.

What BESS Experts is not

  • Not an EPC company and not a project competitor
  • Not a sales channel for one manufacturer only
  • Not pay to play content without technical depth

BESS Experts is a media platform first. EPC work and project execution are done separately through other entities so that this platform can stay neutral and open to everyone.
